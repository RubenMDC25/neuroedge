Compassionate Fibromyalgia Care in Murrieta, CA

Fibromyalgia is a complex chronic condition characterized by widespread pain, fatigue, sleep disturbances, and cognitive difficulties. At NeuroEdge Chiropractic, we understand the challenges of living with fibromyalgia, and we offer a gentle, nervous-system–focused approach to help reduce symptoms and improve quality of life.

Understanding Fibromyalgia

Fibromyalgia affects how your nervous system processes pain signals, leading to:

  • Widespread musculoskeletal pain and tender points
  • Chronic fatigue and poor sleep quality
  • "Fibro fog" — difficulty concentrating and memory issues
  • Heightened sensitivity to touch, temperature, light, and sound
  • Digestive issues, headaches, and mood disturbances

While there's no cure for fibromyalgia, the right combination of therapies can significantly reduce symptoms and improve daily function.

Our Fibromyalgia Treatment Approach

At NeuroEdge Chiropractic, we use gentle, nervous-system–focused therapies tailored to the unique sensitivities of fibromyalgia patients:

Gentle Chiropractic Care

Low-force adjustments help restore proper spinal alignment and reduce nerve irritation without aggravating sensitive tissues. Our gentle approach focuses on calming the nervous system rather than traditional forceful adjustments.

Nervous System Regulation

We use techniques specifically designed to help regulate the autonomic nervous system, which is often dysregulated in fibromyalgia patients. This can help improve sleep quality and reduce overall pain sensitivity.

SoftWave Therapy

Low-intensity acoustic wave therapy can help reduce inflammation, improve circulation, and promote tissue healing without the discomfort of deep pressure or manipulation.

Sleep and Lifestyle Coaching

We provide guidance on sleep hygiene, stress management, pacing activities, and dietary considerations that can help reduce flare frequency and severity.

Trigger Point and Tender Point Management

Gentle soft tissue techniques help address specific areas of tenderness and muscle tension without causing additional pain or sensitivity.

Benefits of Nervous-System–Focused Care for Fibromyalgia

  • Reduced Pain Sensitivity – Calming the nervous system can help lower overall pain levels
  • Improved Sleep Quality – Better nervous system regulation supports deeper, more restful sleep
  • Fewer Flares – Consistent care can help reduce the frequency and intensity of symptom flares
  • Enhanced Function – Gradual improvements in daily activities and quality of life
  • Gentle, Safe Approach – No forceful adjustments or aggressive treatments
  • Personalized Care – Treatment is adapted to your sensitivity levels and specific symptoms

What to Expect

Fibromyalgia care requires patience and consistency. We start slowly, with very gentle techniques, and gradually build a treatment plan that your body can tolerate. Many patients notice improvements in sleep quality first, followed by gradual reductions in pain and fatigue over time.
