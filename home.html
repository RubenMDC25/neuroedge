How We Can Help You

SoftWave® Therapy

Jump-start your body's repair for stubborn pain

Best for: Shoulder, knee, plantar fascia, elbow, hip bursitis/tendinitis, chronic strains — plus many spine/nerve issues (disc irritation, sciatica, SI dysfunction, chronic low back).

Why it works: Focused acoustic waves boost micro-circulation, modulate inflammation, and help activate local repair cells so tissue can recover.

You'll feel: Firm tapping pulses during a 10–15 minute session; intensity stays comfortable. No downtime.

Timeline: Acute problems can shift quickly; chronic cases often benefit from a short series (commonly 4–8 visits) for lasting change.

Spinal Decompression

Relieve disc pressure, calm nerve pain

Best for: Disc bulges/herniations, sciatica/radiculopathy, spinal stenosis, chronic low-back or neck pain after sitting/standing.

Why it works: Computer-controlled traction gently unloads the spine, lowering intradiscal pressure to promote hydration and reduce irritation on nearby nerves.

You'll feel: A comfortable, cyclical stretch; most sessions run 12–20 minutes with you lying relaxed and strapped in.

Timeline: Many patients notice easing within the first few weeks; durable change typically requires a focused series with reassessments to guide progress.

Chiropractic Care

Restore motion, reduce pain, move better

Best for: Neck and back pain, headaches/migraines, mid-back/rib pain, posture strain, pregnancy-related discomfort, general maintenance/wellness.

Why it works: Precise, low-force adjustments restore joint mechanics, which can reduce local inflammation/nerve irritation and improve movement patterns.

You'll feel: Techniques tailored to you—traditional hands-on or instrument-assisted, plus mobility and strength work as needed.

Timeline: Acute sprains/strains often improve over a few visits; longer-standing issues do best with a staged plan (restore → stabilize → maintain).
