Comprehensive Auto Accident Injury Treatment in Murrieta, CA

Being in an auto accident can be traumatic, and the injuries sustained often don't show up immediately. Many people feel fine right after a collision, only to experience pain, stiffness, and other symptoms days or even weeks later. At NeuroEdge Chiropractic, we specialize in evaluating and treating auto accident injuries to help you recover fully and avoid long-term complications.

Common Auto Accident Injuries We Treat

  • Whiplash – Sudden jerking motion of the head and neck causing strain to muscles, ligaments, and joints
  • Neck and Back Pain – Soft tissue damage, disc injuries, or spinal misalignments
  • Headaches and Migraines – Often resulting from neck trauma or concussion
  • Shoulder and Upper Back Pain – Impact injuries to the shoulder girdle and thoracic spine
  • Numbness and Tingling – Nerve irritation or compression from spinal trauma
  • Disc Herniations or Bulges – Impact can cause discs to rupture or press on nerves
  • Concussion and Mild Traumatic Brain Injury – Neurological symptoms requiring careful evaluation

Why Immediate Evaluation Is Critical

After an auto accident, your body releases adrenaline and endorphins, which can mask pain and injury. Additionally, soft tissue injuries and spinal misalignments may not be immediately apparent. Delaying treatment can lead to:

  • Chronic pain and long-term disability
  • Scar tissue formation and reduced range of motion
  • Difficulty with insurance claims and documentation
  • Development of compensatory movement patterns leading to further injury

That's why it's essential to get evaluated by a chiropractor experienced in auto accident injuries as soon as possible — even if you feel fine.
