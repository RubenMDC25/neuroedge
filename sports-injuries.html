Expert Sports Injury Treatment in Murrieta, CA

Whether you're a weekend warrior or a competitive athlete, sports injuries can sideline you from the activities you love. At NeuroEdge Chiropractic, we specialize in treating athletic injuries with a comprehensive approach that focuses on rapid recovery, performance optimization, and injury prevention.

Common Sports Injuries We Treat

Our sports injury treatment protocols address a wide range of athletic conditions:

  • Sprains and Strains – Ligament and muscle injuries from sudden movements or overuse
  • Tendonitis – Inflammation of tendons in shoulders, elbows, knees, and ankles
  • Runner's Knee – Patellofemoral pain syndrome affecting runners and cyclists
  • Rotator Cuff Injuries – Shoulder pain and weakness from overhead activities
  • IT Band Syndrome – Hip and knee pain from repetitive motion
  • Plantar Fasciitis – Heel and foot pain affecting runners and athletes
  • Shin Splints – Lower leg pain from running and high-impact activities
  • Muscle Tears and Pulls – Acute and chronic muscle injuries
  • Joint Dysfunction – Reduced mobility and performance from joint restrictions

Our Sports Injury Treatment Approach

We use advanced therapies and proven rehabilitation techniques to help athletes recover faster and stronger:

Comprehensive Sports Assessment

We evaluate your injury, biomechanics, movement patterns, and sport-specific demands to create a targeted treatment plan.

Chiropractic Sports Medicine

Specialized adjustments restore proper joint function, reduce pain, and optimize biomechanical performance for your specific sport.

SoftWave Therapy for Athletes

Our cutting-edge SoftWave Tissue Regeneration Therapy accelerates healing by stimulating blood flow, reducing inflammation, and activating your body's natural repair mechanisms. This non-invasive treatment helps athletes recover faster from both acute injuries and chronic conditions.

Performance Rehabilitation

Sport-specific exercises and functional training to rebuild strength, improve flexibility, and prevent re-injury when you return to competition.

Return-to-Play Strategy

We develop a gradual progression plan to safely return you to your sport at full capacity, reducing the risk of setbacks.
