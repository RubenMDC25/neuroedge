Effective Treatment for Shoulder and Hip Pain in Murrieta, CA

Shoulder and hip pain can significantly limit your daily activities, making it difficult to lift objects, exercise, sleep comfortably, or even walk without discomfort. At NeuroEdge Chiropractic, we specialize in treating shoulder and hip pain by addressing the underlying biomechanical and neurological causes.

Common Causes of Shoulder Pain

  • Rotator Cuff Injuries – Tears, strains, or tendonitis in the shoulder muscles and tendons
  • Frozen Shoulder (Adhesive Capsulitis) – Stiffness and reduced range of motion
  • Impingement Syndrome – Compression of tendons or bursa in the shoulder joint
  • Arthritis – Degenerative changes causing pain and limited mobility
  • Bursitis – Inflammation of the fluid-filled sacs cushioning the shoulder joint
  • Referred Pain from Neck – Nerve irritation in the cervical spine causing shoulder symptoms

Common Causes of Hip Pain

  • Hip Bursitis – Inflammation of the bursa on the outside of the hip
  • Hip Flexor Strain – Overuse or injury to the muscles that lift the thigh
  • Labral Tears – Damage to the cartilage surrounding the hip socket
  • Arthritis – Wear-and-tear or inflammatory arthritis in the hip joint
  • Sciatica and Referred Pain – Nerve irritation from the lower back causing hip pain
  • IT Band Syndrome – Tightness or inflammation of the iliotibial band
  • Sacroiliac (SI) Joint Dysfunction – Problems with the joint connecting the spine to the pelvis

How We Treat Shoulder and Hip Pain

Comprehensive Biomechanical Assessment

We evaluate your shoulder or hip mechanics, identify compensatory movement patterns, and assess how your spine and nervous system are contributing to the problem.

Chiropractic Adjustments

Adjustments to the spine, shoulder, or hip restore proper joint alignment and reduce nerve irritation. This helps improve mobility and reduce pain.

SoftWave Therapy

For chronic or stubborn shoulder and hip pain, SoftWave Therapy uses acoustic waves to reduce inflammation, break down scar tissue, and accelerate healing. Learn more about SoftWave Therapy.

Class IV Laser Therapy

High-powered laser therapy reduces inflammation and pain in the shoulder or hip joint, promoting faster recovery.

Targeted Rehabilitation and Strengthening

We provide customized exercises to strengthen stabilizing muscles, improve flexibility, and correct movement patterns that contribute to pain.

Postural and Ergonomic Guidance

We help you identify lifestyle factors or work habits that may be contributing to your shoulder or hip pain and provide strategies to correct them.
