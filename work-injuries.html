Comprehensive Work Injury Treatment in Murrieta, CA

Work-related injuries can happen in any occupation — from physical labor to desk work. At NeuroEdge Chiropractic, we provide thorough evaluation, effective treatment, and proper documentation to help you recover from work injuries and safely return to your job.

Common Work Injuries We Treat

We specialize in treating a wide range of occupational injuries:

  • Lifting Injuries – Back strains, disc injuries, and muscle tears from improper lifting or heavy loads
  • Repetitive Strain Injuries (RSI) – Carpal tunnel syndrome, tendonitis, and overuse injuries from repeated motions
  • Desk-Related Injuries – Neck pain, upper back tension, headaches, and postural strain from prolonged sitting and computer work
  • Slip and Fall Accidents – Sprains, strains, and spinal injuries from workplace falls
  • Whiplash and Impact Injuries – Neck and back injuries from workplace accidents
  • Shoulder and Upper Extremity Injuries – Rotator cuff strains, shoulder impingement, elbow tendonitis

Our Work Injury Treatment Approach

We provide comprehensive care designed to get you back to work safely and efficiently:

Thorough Evaluation and Documentation

We perform a complete assessment of your injury, functional limitations, and work demands. All findings are properly documented for workers' compensation claims and employer reports.

Targeted Treatment

Depending on your specific injury, we may use:

  • Chiropractic adjustments to restore proper alignment and function
  • SoftWave Therapy to accelerate healing and reduce inflammation
  • Rehabilitative exercises to rebuild strength and prevent re-injury
  • Ergonomic coaching to prevent future problems

Return-to-Work Planning

We develop a gradual, safe plan to help you return to full work duties. This may include temporary work modifications, progressive loading, and functional capacity assessments.

Injury Prevention Education

We teach proper body mechanics, lifting techniques, workstation setup, and movement strategies to help prevent future work-related injuries.

Workers' Compensation and Insurance

We work with workers' compensation insurance and provide all necessary documentation for your claim, including:

  • Initial injury evaluation reports
  • Progress notes and treatment records
  • Work status updates and restrictions
  • Return-to-work recommendations
  • Permanent and Stationary (P&S) reports when applicable

Why Choose NeuroEdge for Work Injury Care?

  • Experience with Workers' Comp – We understand the documentation and reporting requirements
  • Fast, Effective Treatment – Advanced therapies help you recover quickly
  • Return-to-Work Focus – We're committed to getting you back to your job safely
  • Comprehensive Care – We address the injury and teach prevention strategies
  • Professional Documentation – All reports are thorough, timely, and professionally prepared
