Gentle Chiropractic Care for Pregnancy and Children in Murrieta, CA

At NeuroEdge Chiropractic, we provide specialized, gentle chiropractic care for expectant mothers and children of all ages. Our low-force techniques are safe, effective, and adapted to the unique needs of growing bodies and changing physiology.

Chiropractic Care During Pregnancy

Pregnancy brings profound physical changes that can lead to discomfort and pain. Chiropractic care during pregnancy can help:

  • Reduce Back and Hip Pain – As your belly grows and posture changes, the spine and pelvis can become misaligned, causing pain and discomfort
  • Optimize Pelvic Balance – Proper pelvic alignment may support optimal fetal positioning and easier labor
  • Relieve Sciatica – Nerve compression from postural changes can cause shooting leg pain
  • Improve Sleep Quality – Reduced pain and better alignment can help you sleep more comfortably
  • Support Overall Wellness – A balanced nervous system supports better overall health for you and your baby

Prenatal Chiropractic Techniques

We use gentle, pregnancy-safe techniques including:

  • Webster Technique – A specific sacral adjustment designed to optimize pelvic balance
  • Low-force adjustments adapted for pregnancy comfort
  • Special pregnancy pillows for safe, comfortable positioning

Pediatric Chiropractic Care

Children benefit from gentle chiropractic care to support healthy growth, development, and movement. We provide age-appropriate care for:

  • Newborns – Gentle assessments after birth to address any strain from the birthing process
  • Infants – Support for proper head and neck control, latch issues, and overall comfort
  • Toddlers and Children – Care for growing pains, postural development, sports injuries, and everyday bumps and falls
  • Adolescents – Support for posture, sports performance, backpack strain, and rapid growth changes

Conditions We Help With in Children

  • Postural imbalances and growing pains
  • Sports injuries and athletic performance
  • Torticollis (head tilt) in infants
  • Developmental milestones and coordination
  • Headaches and neck pain
  • Backpack-related strain

Gentle, Kid-Friendly Techniques

Pediatric adjustments use minimal force — often just the pressure of a fingertip. Children typically find adjustments comfortable and may even fall asleep during treatment. We make every visit positive, educational, and fun for kids.
